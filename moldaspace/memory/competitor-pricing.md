## Key Patterns Discovered

### Free Trial Strategies
- **Most generous**: REimagine Home (3 free designs)
- **Minimal**: RoomGPT (1 free generation)
- **No free trial**: Collov AI, Interior AI (premium positioning)

### Pricing Models
1. **Credit/Photo-based**: REimagine Home, Collov AI
2. **Monthly design limits**: Interior AI
3. **Unknown/Minimal**: RoomGPT

### Price Ranges (Monthly)
- **Entry level**: $14-19/month
- **Professional**: $29-49/month  
- **Enterprise**: $79-127/month

### Paywall Approaches
- **Soft paywall**: REimagine Home (3 free then upgrade)
- **Hard paywall**: Collov AI, Interior AI (no free trial)
- **Freemium unclear**: RoomGPT (may be permanently free with ads)

## Competitive Insights for MoldaSpace

### 1. Free Trial Strategy
- **Best practice**: Offer 3-5 free renders (matches REimagine Home)
- **Differentiation**: Consider offering more free renders than competitors

### 2. Pricing Positioning
- **Sweet spot**: $29-49/month appears to be the professional tier
- **Entry point**: Consider $19-24/month to undercut Collov AI
- **Enterprise**: $79-99/month for high-volume users

### 3. Paywall Approach
- **Recommendation**: Soft paywall with generous free trial
- **After paywall**: Show low-res previews or watermarked results
- **Upgrade trigger**: When users want to download/save results

### 4. Credit System vs Fixed Plans
- **Credits**: More flexible, appeals to occasional users
- **Fixed limits**: Simpler, better for predictable revenue
- **Hybrid**: Best of both worlds

### 5. Key Differentiators Observed
- **REimagine Home**: Real products integration, conversation-based design
- **Collov AI**: Real estate focus, virtual staging specialization  
- **Interior AI**: Video/VR features, ultra-high volume plans
- **RoomGPT**: Simplicity, possibly free forever

## Recommendations for MoldaSpace

1. **Free Trial**: 5 free renders (more generous than competitors)
2. **Entry Plan**: $24/month (50 renders) - positioned between REimagine Essential and Collov Standard
3. **Pro Plan**: $39/month (200 renders) - competitive with REimagine Pro
4. **Enterprise**: $79/month (unlimited) - matches Interior AI Premium territory
5. **Paywall**: Soft approach - show watermarked/low-res after free trial ends
6. **Unique Value**: Focus on architectural accuracy and Brazilian market
