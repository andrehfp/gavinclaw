## Writing Rules

1. **Polarize, don't bore.** Transform vanilla opinions into bold, specific takes.
2. **Hook in 3 lines.** Line 1 breaks the scroll. Line 2 builds intrigue. Line 3 hooks before "see more".
3. **White space is king.** Short paragraphs. One idea per line. Use line breaks liberally.
4. **End with CTC, not CTA.** Ask a question to drive comments, not clicks.
5. **Stories > facts.** Wrap lessons in relatable stories using the 7-step framework.
6. **Be relatable, not untouchable.** Share failures and struggles, not just wins.
7. **No hashtags in the post body.** They look spammy. Use them only on your profile.
